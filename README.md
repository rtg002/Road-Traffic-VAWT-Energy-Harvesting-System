 🌬️ Road Traffic VAWT Energy Harvesting System

A sustainable energy solution that uses a **Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT)** to harness wind turbulence generated by moving vehicles on roads and highways, converting it into usable electrical energy.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on capturing **wasted kinetic energy from road traffic airflow** and converting it into electrical power using a **Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT)**.

The system is designed for **low wind-speed conditions**, making it ideal for deployment along highways and urban roads where vehicle-induced airflow is frequent but irregular.

An **Arduino-based control system** manages power generation and battery charging, making the setup efficient and automated.

## ⚙️ Working Principle

1. Moving vehicles create airflow turbulence along roads.
2. The **VAWT blades rotate** due to this airflow.
3. The turbine drives a **DC generator**, producing electrical energy.
4. Generated power is regulated through a **charging circuit**.
5. Arduino monitors voltage/current and controls charging.
6. Energy is stored in a **battery system**.
7. (Optional) IoT module sends real-time data to cloud/dashboard.
